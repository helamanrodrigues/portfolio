Arc of Triomphe - France

The Arc de Triomphe is located in the middle of Paris, you can go up and see the whole city from up there, I recommend it at sunset, when you can see the sun going down and the Eiffel Tower being turned on, it's a great experience and the view is very beautiful and unforgettable.

Camdem Town - England

Camdem Town is in the center of London, there you will find everything you need, it is a very beautiful place, the stores are attractive and the products are unique, the food is delicious, if you go there you don't want to leave.

Dublin - Ireland

Dublin is a quiet and safe city, there are many Pubs, great place to meet friends and have fun, an university city, so you'll find a lot of young people and entertainments, it is also very historic, so you can't leave without learn from the history of Ireland.
