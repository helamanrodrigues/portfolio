The Boy Who Lived... In Soda Springs

Once upon a time there was a boy with a lightning shaped scar on his forehead. He grew up with his aunt uncle and cousin, who all treated him with petulance. One day a giant of a man came and told him about the truths that had been kept from him all his life. He was a wizard, as were his parents before they had died fighting against an evil wizard. After meeting the kind half-giant, the boy's life improved. He went to a school filled with other wizard children who, for the most part, all treated him with kindness.

However, his struggles were not all eradicated when he joined the wizarding community. For the evil wizard who killed his parents was not completely dead. For six year the boy went to school, learning more about spell craft and the wizarding community, and each year he had adventures. He fought against trolls, basilisks, giant spiders, a sphinx, blast ended skrewts, and of course, dark wizards. After six years of schooling, he leaves school to go on a quest with his two best friends in an effort to destroy the dark wizard how killed his parents. What you probably don't know about this famous wizard who you want to call Harry, is that he is a real person who lives in Soda Springs.
