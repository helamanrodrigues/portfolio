Target Audience

They are important to most aspects of day to day life, including aviation, boating, other modes of transportation, farming, tourism, sports, etc. Without accurate of this site people involved in activities like the ones I've listed may end up in dangerous situations they were unprepared for and end up injured or worse. Pilots need to know the weather to plan their flights, sailors need to know what the weather will be like to plan their activities, and farmers need to know what the weather will be like to help them plan watering, fertilizer and pesticide application, and harvest activities, to name a few. In short this is my audience, the workers that need to know the weather to avoid dangerous situations.

  • Age: Between 18-45 years old
  • Experience: Any professional work
  • Income: Depend of the profession
  • Motivation: As the purpose stated, the motivation is to become a site that can help the workers to avoid dangerous situations with the weather (flights, activities), so they can have all the necesary information to plan in advance their upcomming calendar.
  • How: Mostly intended to be mobile friendly, since workers schedule their activities on their phone calendar. Not limited to mobile, but intended to being accesible on any PC, laptop.

Personas

Mark Daniels

  • Age: 24 years

  • Profession: Athlete

  • Bio: Mark is a athlete. He often uses his bicycle to commute to his local for training. He loves outdoor activities - hiking, biking, and camping. It is important to Mark that he able to access an easy to find and accurate weather forecast so he can plan his commute and daily activities according.

  • Goals:
    1. Easily able to scan weather forecast and other weather related reports.
    2. Ability to view daily, weekly, and biweekly forecast.
    3. View weather forecasts snapshot on one screen.

  • Frustrations:
    1. Current weather app is inaccurat, he often has to check 2 or more apps to get an accurate forecast.
    2. Not able to monitor weather for multiple locations.
    3. Overcomplicated interface of weather app makes it difficult to get a quick scan of the weather.

Crystal Evan

  • Age: 33 years

  • Profession: Event and Wedding Planner

  • Bio: Crystal is a busy event and wedding planner. Her clients are relying on her to plan the perfect day. It is very important to her that she is able to get an accurate, easy to glance at the weather forecast. She also uses the weather so she knows the best time to book camping sites for her own personal enjoyment.

  • Goals:
    1. Be able to quickly view weekly forecast so she is able to plan events for client outings.
    2. View closures and nearby locations.
    3. Easily search for weather for several locations.

  • Frustrations:
    1. Unable to check for severe weather alerts, road closures, and nearby location closures due to weather.
    2. Current weather app drains her battery quickly.
    3. Inability to view nearby locations.

Scenarios

  1. Mark wants to plan commute and activities his next friday. How will be the weather in this day?
  2. Crystal have new client that wants to marry in April on the south of the US, she needs to check the weather and the place for this month to check the best option for her client.
  3. Mark want to take a walk in his lunch time, he decides to check the weather to do that.
  4. Crystal is planning to take some vacations for this year, but she needs to know what is the best weather in the year and the place to decide her vacations.
  5. Mark have a championship to the next week, he wants to plan his training for the next 6 days and need to know the weather for this time.
